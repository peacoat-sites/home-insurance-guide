Coverage Recommended Amount Notes
Flood coverage required: Standard homeowners insurance does NOT cover flooding. You must purchase a separate policy through the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP) — current average cost is $700–$900/year. Visit floodsmart.gov to find a policy.
Earthquake coverage: Standard policies exclude earthquake damage. Purchase a separate earthquake policy through your insurer or the CEA (California Earthquake Authority).
Annual review reminder: Review your coverage every year — replacement costs have risen 25–40% since 2019. What was adequate coverage three years ago may leave you underinsured today.
